Hair trimming or clipping

This involves using clippers or scissors to trim the pet's hair to a specific length, either for functional or aesthetic purposes. This can help to keep the pet's coat looking neat and prevent matting or tangling.

Bathing and grooming

This involves using pet-friendly shampoos and conditioners to clean the pet's coat and skin, and may also include a blow-dry or towel dry.

Haircuts and styling

Some pet owners may want their pet to have a specific haircut or style, such as a breed-specific cut or a creative, unique style.

Dietary Counseling

We will provide guidance regarding your pet's nutritional needs for each life stage, including dietary requirements for growth, weight maintenance and performance.

Boarding

We provide pet boarding as a service to our clients. All boarders receive personal attention from our caring staff. Pets may be dropped off and picked up during regular business hours Monday through Friday.

Skin and coat treatments

Pet salons may offer a range of treatments to improve the health and appearance of the pet's coat and skin, such as deep conditioning treatments, hot oil treatments, and flea and tick treatments.

Nail's Cut

Trimming a pet's nails is important for their health and well-being, as overgrown nails can cause discomfort and even lead to infection or injury. When a pet's nails are too long, they can interfere with their ability to walk and cause pain when they make contact with hard surfaces.
